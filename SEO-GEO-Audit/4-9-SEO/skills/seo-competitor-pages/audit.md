- Competitors rank with dedicated landing pages per furniture category (lobby, guestroom, restaurant, outdoor)
- Most competitors maintain active blogs covering hospitality design trends, driving long-tail traffic
- Competitors have significant backlink profiles from hospitality trade publications (Hospitality Design, Hotel Management, etc.)
- DMD's product catalog depth (180+ items) is competitive, but zero content marketing means no keyword coverage

- Do not attempt to compete on head terms immediately — focus on long-tail hospitality furniture keywords with lower competition
