## Executive Summary

DMD Furnishing has near-zero organic visibility for its target keywords. A `site:dmdfurnishing.com` search returns only **3 indexed pages** (homepage, /products, /website-policies). The remaining 722 URLs in the sitemap -- including all 6 blog posts, all product category pages, and all 636 product detail pages -- appear to not be indexed or ranking for any measurable queries. The SERPs for every target keyword are dominated by established competitors with 20-30+ year track records and robust content ecosystems. DMD does not appear in any SERP results for any of the 12 researched keywords.

**Critical finding:** The site has strong on-page SEO foundations (schema markup, meta tags, blog content) but is functionally invisible in search. This is likely an indexation or domain authority problem rather than an on-page issue.

## 6. Indexation Crisis -- Root Cause Analysis

### The Core Problem

DMD has **725 URLs in its sitemap** but only **3 pages appear indexed in Google**. This is a 99.6% non-indexation rate. Until this is resolved, no keyword strategy will produce results.

### Likely Causes (Ranked by Probability)

1. **Domain is too new / lacks authority** -- Google may be crawling but not indexing low-authority pages
2. **Thin content on product pages** -- 636 individual product pages may have too little unique text
3. **Crawl budget exhaustion** -- 725 URLs on a new domain may overwhelm crawl allocation
4. **Staging vs production confusion** -- Canonical URLs point to dmdfurnishing.com but the site may have been primarily accessed at dmdredesign.netlify.app, splitting signals
5. **No backlinks** -- Without external links, Google has little reason to deeply crawl the site
6. **robots.txt or meta noindex** -- Possible technical block (needs verification)

### Immediate Actions Required

1. **Verify Google Search Console** is connected to dmdfurnishing.com (not the Netlify subdomain)
2. **Submit sitemap** manually through Search Console
3. **Request indexing** for the 6 blog posts and top 10 product category pages
4. **Check robots.txt** for any disallow directives
5. **Verify canonical tags** are consistent (all pointing to dmdfurnishing.com)
6. **Build initial backlinks** -- Even 5-10 quality links would dramatically improve crawl priority
