# Skill: seo (Master Summary) — dmdredesign.netlify.app
**Date:** 2026-04-09 | **Status:** COMPLETE

## Summary
Master SEO summary synthesizing all sub-audit findings for DMD Furnishing's redesigned website. Overall SEO Health Score is approximately 55/100, reflecting strong code quality (72-82) but critically low domain authority (18-22). The site faces a three-layer problem: a deployment crisis with canonical domain mismatch, infrastructure gaps in indexation and performance, and near-zero business presence in search.

## Key Findings
- **Code Quality (72-82/100):** Next.js 15 SSR implementation is solid; robots.txt well-configured; AI crawler access enabled; structured data present on key pages
- **Authority Score (18-22/100):** Domain has virtually no backlink profile; zero referring domains of substance; no brand SERP presence
- **Deployment Crisis:** Canonical URLs point to dmdfurnishing.com but site is served from dmdredesign.netlify.app — search engines see conflicting signals
- **Indexation Failure:** Only 3 of 725 total URLs are indexed by Google; sitemap contains 53 dead URLs pointing to non-existent product paths
- **Core Web Vitals:** Desktop acceptable (LCP ~2.5s); mobile failing badly (LCP ~11s, well above 2.5s threshold)
- **Content Gaps:** 180+ product pages have thin content (<150 words); no blog, no resource center, no topical authority content
- **Local SEO:** No Google Business Profile; no local citations; invisible in map results
- **Schema Markup:** Present but incomplete — sameAs links point to broken social profiles (LinkedIn 404, no YouTube)

## Recommendations
- **Immediate (Week 1):** Fix canonical domain configuration; remove 53 dead URLs from sitemap; submit corrected sitemap to Google Search Console
- **Short-term (Month 1):** Resolve mobile CWV failures (optimize images, reduce JS bundle); create Google Business Profile; fix LinkedIn URL
- **Medium-term (Month 2-3):** Build content strategy with blog targeting hospitality furniture keywords; enrich product page descriptions to 300+ words; begin backlink outreach to hospitality industry directories
- **Long-term (Month 4-6):** Establish topical authority through case studies and project showcases; pursue backlinks from hospitality trade publications; target competitor keyword gaps
