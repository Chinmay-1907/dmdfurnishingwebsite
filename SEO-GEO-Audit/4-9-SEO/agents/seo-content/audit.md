## E-E-A-T Breakdown

### Experience (17/20)
**Strengths:**
- Real project portfolio with before/after photography and specifications (room count, scope, category)
- Process descriptions derived from actual manufacturing workflow (6-step process with week-level timelines)
- Blog content demonstrates hands-on manufacturing knowledge (e.g., "HPL over an MDF carcass," drawer slide ratings, edge-banding details)
- Contact page uses OTP verification — signals real business operations, not a content farm
- Physical address (56 Leonard St, Foxboro MA) consistent across all pages, footer, schema

**Gaps:**
- No individual author bylines — all content authored by "DMD Furnishing" (organization). Personal experience signals from a named founder, lead designer, or project manager would strengthen Experience
- No client testimonials or named references on any page
- No case study narratives with client quotes or named properties

### Expertise (21/25)
**Strengths:**
- Blog articles demonstrate deep domain expertise: BIFMA standards, CAL 133 fire codes, ADA compliance, NFPA, construction accounting terminology (BOQ, RFQ, FF&E vs OS&E depreciation)
- External authority references: AHLA, AIA, BIFMA, NFPA, ADA.gov, CMAA, Forest Products Laboratory, Hospitality Design magazine, National Restaurant Association, Architectural Woodwork Institute
- Technical vocabulary used naturally: "rub count," "MDF carcass," "edge-banded laminate," "commercial-grade hardware," "soft-close undermount slides"
- Material comparison table (HPL vs Veneer vs Solid Wood) with 8 attributes — highly quotable and AI-citation-ready
- Process timeline with specific week ranges and deliverables per phase

**Gaps:**
- No mention of team credentials, certifications, or years of experience
- No industry award or membership mentions (AHLA member? BIFMA tested?)
- Blog articles lack data citations for specific claims (e.g., "15-25% of total construction costs" on homepage FAQ — no source)

### Authoritativeness (19/25)
**Strengths:**
- Rich structured data: Organization, LocalBusiness, WebSite, Article, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, HowTo, Service, CollectionPage schemas implemented across all pages
- Canonical URLs set correctly on all pages
- SpeakableSpecification on homepage (AI assistant readiness)
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across site: footer, contact page, CTA sections, schema
- Social media profiles linked (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest) in both footer and schema sameAs

**Gaps:**
- All social profiles use generic URLs (facebook.com/dmdfurnishing) — need verification these are live, active profiles with engagement
- No Google Business Profile link or reviews integration
- No third-party trust signals: BBB, Houzz, industry association membership badges
- No backlink-generating content assets (downloadable PDFs, spec sheets, planning tools)
- Blog articles all dated March 28, 2026 — no publication cadence signal; looks like a content batch

### Trustworthiness (25/30)
**Strengths:**
- Physical business address displayed on every page via footer and CTA blocks
- Direct phone number and email prominently displayed
- OTP-verified contact form prevents spam and signals legitimate operation
- Website policies page linked in footer
- HTTPS enforced; reCAPTCHA v3 on contact form
- No fabricated statistics — trust bar stats (rooms renovated, completed projects, products) are dynamically computed from actual project and catalog data
- Consistent pricing signal ($$$) in LocalBusiness schema
- Opening hours specified in schema

**Gaps:**
- No privacy policy, terms of service, or cookie notice visible (only generic "Website Policies" link)
- No warranty or guarantee language on service pages
- No SSL/security badges on contact or quote forms
- No client logos or "trusted by" section

## Issues by Severity

### CRITICAL (Must Fix)
1. **Thin content on /products and /products/[category] pages** — Auto-generated catalog with <150 words. Add 300+ words per page with intros, buying guides, FAQ.
2. **No individual author attribution** — All content by "DMD Furnishing" organization. Add named author with credentials.
3. **Meta descriptions too long on 4+ pages** — Homepage (186), Services (200), FF&E blog (200). Trim to 150-160 chars.

### HIGH (Should Fix Soon)
4. **All blog posts share same publication date** — No content freshness signal. Stagger dates or add dateModified.
5. **No client testimonials or case study narratives** — Missing key trust signal for B2B.
6. **About page lacks founding date, team info, credentials** — Weakens Experience signal.
7. **Homepage H1 creative, not keyword-optimized** — "Designed. Manufactured. Delivered."
8. **No downloadable resources** — No PDFs, spec sheets, or planning tools.

### MEDIUM (Should Address)
9. Product category pages need unique content + FAQ
10. No Google Business Profile integration
11. Blog articles don't cross-link to each other
12. Contact page missing map, hours, response time
13. No newsletter or blog subscription
14. Footer missing /blog link

### LOW (Nice to Have)
15. No video content on any page
16. Blog articles share same OG image
17. No visible "last updated" dates on blog posts
18. Individual project pages not audited
