## 1. Citability Analysis (Score: 48/100)

Citability measures how likely AI systems are to extract and cite content from the site when answering user queries.

### Methodology

Four pages analyzed: Homepage, About, Services, Blog (What Is FF&E). For each, the top 5 substantive content blocks (excluding nav/footer) were scored on:

- Answer Quality (30%): Opens with clear 1-2 sentence answer
- Self-Containment (25%): Understandable without context
- Structural Readability (20%): Question-based heading, lists/tables
- Statistical Density (15%): Specific %, $, named studies
- Uniqueness (10%): First-party research or original analysis
- Optimal passage length target: 134-167 words

### Wikipedia Analysis
- Wikipedia API returned 8 results for "DMD Furnishing" -- all are false positives
- Results relate to: DMD (dental degree), DMD (Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy), DMD (Serbian company), DMD (Thai entertainment)
- Zero results reference DMD Furnishing or commercial furniture
- This is expected for a small B2B company with no Wikipedia notability criteria met

### Reddit Analysis
- Zero mentions of "DMD Furnishing" on Reddit
- No threads, comments, or discussions about the company
- B2B commercial furniture has minimal Reddit footprint in general
- Opportunity: Participate in r/hospitality, r/interiordesign, r/commercialrealestate

### YouTube Analysis
- No YouTube channel for DMD Furnishing
- YouTube search for "DMD Furnishing" returns unrelated results (generic furniture videos, dental content)
- Brand name collision: "DMD" triggers Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy and dental degree content
- Opportunity: Create a channel with project walkthroughs, material guides, installation timelapses

### LinkedIn Analysis
- https://www.linkedin.com/company/dmdfurnishing returns HTTP 404
- Company page either never created, was removed, or uses a different URL slug
- LinkedIn is the primary B2B discovery platform -- this is a critical gap
- Partial credit (8/25) given because LinkedIn pages can be created immediately

### Brand Name Collision Problem
"DMD" as a three-letter abbreviation has significant collision with:
1. **DMD (Doctor of Medicine in Dentistry / Doctor of Dental Medicine)** -- extremely high search volume
2. **DMD (Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy)** -- major medical condition with massive content footprint
3. **DMD (Digital Micromirror Device)** -- Texas Instruments technology term
4. **DMD (Thai entertainment company)** -- media company

This collision severely limits discoverability. AI systems queried about "DMD" will almost never surface "DMD Furnishing" without the full brand name qualifier. Recommendation: Always use "DMD Furnishing" as the full entity name, never abbreviate to just "DMD."
