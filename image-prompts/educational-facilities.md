# Educational Facilities — Image Prompts

(Covers both K-12 school and university / higher-ed products. On the live site, school and university merge into the `/products/educational-facilities` route.)

## Setting vocabulary
K-12 classrooms feature bright natural light pouring through large windows, colorful accent walls in primary or pastel tones, whiteboard or chalkboard walls, durable linoleum or polished concrete floors, pinboard walls with artwork, and low storage cubbies scaled for younger students. University and higher-ed spaces include industrial-modern libraries with oak shelving and pendant lighting, dormitory rooms with exposed brick walls and bedside sconces, tiered lecture halls with stadium-style rows, and campus lounges with mid-century lounge chairs and café-style tables.

## Plain-background style
A soft single-tone seamless backdrop in bone white or warm light gray wraps under the product with no visible horizon line. Tent lighting from above and sides creates even, shadow-minimizing coverage. A subtle rim light at the rear separates the piece from the background and defines silhouette edges cleanly.

## Furniture types to add
Teacher's desk / podium, height-adjustable classroom desk, K-12 reading nook cushion seating, student locker bank, art-room easel station, science-lab stool, music-room tiered seating, auditorium theater seat, gymnasium bench, cafeteria banquette, campus café lounge pod, university study carrel (solo), university group-study pod, dorm desk chair (ergonomic), library individual reading chair, lecture-hall tablet-arm chair, student center gaming chair, outdoor campus bench, student-union coffee-bar stool, chapel pew, residence-hall wardrobe unit, pinboard display wall panel.
