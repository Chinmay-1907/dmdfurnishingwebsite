# Lobby Area — Image Prompts

## Setting vocabulary
A grand commercial lobby with double-height ceilings, polished travertine or terrazzo floors, and a sculptural statement pendant chandelier casting warm amber pools of light. Lounge seating clusters orbit low coffee tables, anchored by floor-to-ceiling windows dressed in sheer ivory curtains that diffuse afternoon light into golden haze. A curated art wall, walnut or oak paneling, and brass or bronze accent details complete the atmosphere — equal parts hospitality welcome and architectural statement.

## Plain-background style
Warm bone or soft warm-gray seamless paper backdrop, tent lighting overhead with twin softbox panels flanking the product, and a subtle rim light from behind to separate the piece from the background. Shadows are soft, graded, and intentional — not eliminated. The mood is luxury hospitality catalog, close to Aesop product photography.

## Furniture types to add
Consider staging with: low-profile coffee table, side table, travertine floor vase, curated hardcover books stacked on a surface, woven wool rug, brass floor lamp, ceramic table lamp, abstract sculpture on a plinth, fresh botanicals in an architectural planter, walnut credenza in the background, sheer curtain panel, architectural pendant chandelier, bronze or brass accent tray, stone or marble side pedestal, reception desk (curved or straight), bellhop luggage cart, umbrella stand, magazine rack, coat-check counter, branded welcome sign plinth, live-plant planter/pedestal, key-return dropbox cabinet, lobby directory display plinth, coffee-and-tea station cart, arrival bench with USB charging strip, porter trolley station.
