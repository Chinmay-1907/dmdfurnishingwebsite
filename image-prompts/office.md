# Office — Image Prompts

## Setting vocabulary
The executive corner office features floor-to-ceiling windows casting raking daylight across an oak veneer accent wall, a credenza on polished concrete, and a single potted fiddle-leaf fig grounding the corner. The open-plan workstation zone is a long run of benching lit by a continuous linear pendant above muted wool carpet tiles, acoustic felt panels dividing sight-lines, and a biophilic planter wall at the far end. The boardroom centres on a long rectangular table under a row of matte-black pendant fixtures, with a backlit brand panel behind the presenter's end and a credenza rail along the window wall.

## Plain-background style
A warm-neutral seamless sweep — think Swiss coffee or greige — fills the frame behind the piece, with no shadows bleeding onto the backdrop. Tent lighting from above is supplemented by two off-axis softboxes for even fill and clean rim separation. The palette reads like a Steelcase or Herman Miller product catalog: colour-accurate, tonally quiet, and free of any distraction beyond the furniture itself.

## Furniture types to add
The following product types are absent from the current catalogue and should be considered for future shoots: phone booth / focus pod, height-adjustable sit-stand desk, collaboration pod with integrated display screen, breakout lounge chair, modular storage wall system, mobile whiteboard or tackboard cabinet, video-call booth, hot desk panel unit, acoustic meeting dome, reception bench, mail-room sorting table, treadmill-desk station, mother's room glider chair, mail lockers, and height-adjustable conference table.
